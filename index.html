Our Story

We met in college at Washington University in St. Louis, but have different memories of our first meeting. I (David) remember talking to Jane a few times when we lived in the same dorm sophomore year, but Jane doesn't remember meeting me until introduced by friends senior year (and this may have been for the best...).

We started dating in February of 2009 during our last semester of college and both moved to San Francisco after graduation. Almost 6 years later, I (David) "surprised" Jane by proposing the night before we left for a Hawaiian vacation. I (Jane) said yes (...and I'm positive I won't forget this one)!
